Company overview

Once you met the members of Group 41 you will want to work with them. We are an innovative, fun and developing company. You love to work side by side with our developing team.

Group 41 is a new rapidly growing company, who wants people to have a more reliably access to healthcare information and services. They plan to development and implemental a range of health Applications to achieve this. They are committed to provide a respectful, valued high quality product for their clients, which is easily and safe to use, also effective in meeting their needs and maintaining their privacy.

Group 41 products will be provided with integrity and passion. We currently have one web base and application platform which helps individuals and families navigate the health industry to maintain and keep track of their health, by finding the services, products and information they require. Our application is accessible to people of all abilities. It is also aimed to be translated into several languages. The product will grow with demands based on feedback from clients. It is hoped, services will branch out to other areas soon. Into areas such are dementia care in the home and pet care.

Group 41 is an equal opportunity employer. They provide flexible working arrangement to meet the needs of their employees. Our team approaches work with energy and enthusiasm.

Group 41 is committed to a sustainable community and considers the environmental impact of all that we do.

Group 41 is committed to the Occupational Health and Safety of their employees. They provide a workplace with a culture of safety. They have a workplace culture which allows safety issues to be openly discussed, reported, and addressed. Employees are consisted to be able to work in a matter which enhances their own health and safety while benefiting those around them. Workers are responsible for reporting hazards, incidents and injuries to their employer.

All employees are expected to follow all company policies and protocols when carrying out their duties. These policies are developed to promote a shared responsibility for all staff.
